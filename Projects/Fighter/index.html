Developed in Python using the Pygame framework, this multiplayer fighting game leverages sockets for seamless online play, enabling multiple players to battle in real-time. The core features include dynamic hitbox detection, particle effects, and networking for multiplayer experiences.

Players can register and log in through a dedicated website, where they can track their progress, although player stats are still under development. For now, the login and registration functions are fully operational, allowing users to connect and participate in the game.
